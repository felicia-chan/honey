Visualization 3:

Choice of color scheme: The colors chosen for 2012, 2005, and 1998 are respectively purple, orange, and blue. We chose these colors as they are distinct from one another and do not interfere with red-green colorblindness, as blue/purple and orange are easily distinguishable from one another. The colors are also more muted due to the graph having larger area marks, and therefore we wanted the colors to be lower-saturated so as not to be as much as an eyesore. This specific color scheme was chosen to account for people with color blindness, as there is no red-green confusion found in the colors.
Marks and Channels: The marks for this plot are vertical stacks of lines, channels are vertical lengths and horizontal positions as well as colors and spatial region.
